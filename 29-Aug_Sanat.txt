What is Database?
a collection of pieces of information that is organized and used on a computer

ER Diagram
a graphical representation of entities and their relationships to each other in a database.

Normalization
Database Normalization is a stepwise formal process that allows us to decompose database tables in such a way that both data dependency and update anomalies are minimized.

Denormalization
Denormalization is a database optimization technique in which we add redundant data to one or more tables.
